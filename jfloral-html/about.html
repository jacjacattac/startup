    Jessica Palmer was born and raised in Gilbert, Arizona. She has always loved being creative and making things beautiful. While attending BYU, took a floral design class, which sparked her passion for flowers. She then attended a floral class, where she learned the ins and outs of floral design and the floral business. In 2020 she started J Floral Co. and has been servicing people's flower needs for the past 3 years.

    Jessica is available for all flower needs, but specializes particularly in wedding flowers. She loves helping brides have the perfect day and giving them everything they need. She even did the flowers for her own wedding! She would love to be a part of your special day.
